Mad Sessions

Live recordings for Madrid's music community

As sound engineer alongside Alejandro Martínez, I recorded and mixed these exclusive sessions organized by Mad Cool Festival, showcasing emerging artists from Madrid's vibrant music scene. Our setup captured the raw energy of these live performances while maintaining studio-quality audio.

Mychelle • Mad Session

Recording details: Neumann U87 microphones through Neve preamps, mixed in Pro Tools

Ray Bull • Mad Session

Recording details: Coles 4038 ribbon mics on drums, direct bass DI signal

Recorded at Mad Cool Studios, 2023 | Sound Engineering: Luis Soto & Alejandro Martínez
